Learn the fundamentals of simple and compound interest calculations with practical examples.

Lesson Notes

This lesson covers the basic concepts of simple and compound interest, including formulas and practical applications. Key points include:

  • Simple Interest = (P × R × T)/100
  • Compound Interest = P(1 + R/100)^T - P
  • Difference between simple and compound interest
  • Real-world applications in banking and finance

Practice problems will help reinforce these concepts.
