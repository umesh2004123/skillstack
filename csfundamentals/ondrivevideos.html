Clocks Concepts

Learn the fundamentals of clock problems in aptitude tests with practical examples.

Lesson Notes

This lesson covers the basic concepts of clock problems, including formulas and practical applications. Key points include:

  • Minute hand moves 360° in 60 minutes ⇒ 6° per minute
  • Hour hand moves 360° in 12 hours ⇒ 30° per hour ⇒ 0.5° per minute
  • Relative speed between minute and hour hands: 5.5° per minute
  • Angle between hands formula: |30H - 5.5M|

Practice problems will help reinforce these concepts.
