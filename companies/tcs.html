TCS NQT 2025 Preparation

The ultimate guide to cracking the TCS National Qualifier Test. Foundation, Advanced Coding, and Interview tips.

Exam Pattern

The exam is divided into two major sections: Foundation and Advanced. There is no negative marking.

Section Duration Question Type
Foundation (Numerical, Verbal, Reasoning) 75 Mins Easy / Medium
Advanced (Quant, Reasoning, Coding) 90 Mins Hard / Advanced

Foundation Syllabus

Numerical: LCM/HCF, Divisibility, Probability, Statistics
Verbal: Reading Comprehension, Sentence Completion, Errors
Reasoning: Blood Relations, Coding-Decoding, Series

Advanced Syllabus

Coding: Arrays, Strings, Matrices, Trees (Hard)
Adv. Quant: Permutations, Geometry, Trigonometry
Time Limit: Strict timing for coding questions.
