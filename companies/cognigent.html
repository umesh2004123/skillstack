Cognizant Hiring Process

Important Note: Cognizant has recently updated their hiring process to include more coding and communication assessment.

Cognizant GenC Hiring Process 2025

Phase Description Duration Status
1. Online Assessment Cognitive + Communication Test 100 mins Mandatory
2. Technical Interview Technical skills evaluation 45-60 mins Mandatory
3. HR Interview Final cultural fit assessment 30 mins Mandatory

Detailed Cognizant Online Assessment Pattern

Section Questions Duration Difficulty
Cognitive Test 24 50 mins Medium
Quantitative Aptitude 8 16 mins Easy-Medium
Logical Reasoning 8 16 mins Medium

Communication Test 26 50 mins Medium

Key Points:

  • No negative marking in any section
  • Sectional time limits - cannot switch between sections
  • Communication test includes voice recording for speaking assessment
  • Technical interview includes live coding for technical roles

Preparation Tips

Expert recommendations to help you crack the Cognizant hiring process:

Online Test Strategy
  • Practice with sectional time limits to build speed
  • Attempt easier questions first to secure marks
  • For communication test, practice speaking clearly into microphone
  • Use elimination technique for reasoning questions
Study Approach
  • Focus on accuracy first, then build speed
  • Solve previous year Cognizant question papers
  • Make formula sheets for quick revision
  • Practice English communication daily
Interview Tips
  • Be prepared with your resume projects
  • Practice coding on whiteboard/paper
  • Prepare STAR method for behavioral questions
  • Research about Cognizant's work and values
Communication Test
  • Practice speaking clearly and at moderate pace
  • Record yourself to identify areas for improvement
  • Work on pronunciation of common tech terms
  • Listen to English news/podcasts daily
